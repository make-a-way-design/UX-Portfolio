Inns by the Sea is a small hospitality company based in Carmel-by-the-Sea, CA with four welcoming, classic inns on the California coast. I joined the team as a Front Desk Agent in October, then was promoted to Reservations Specialist in January 2025. As I learned more about the clients, I noticed booking trends and pain points; this project promotes the properties and resolves key booking issues.

Project Overview

The IBTS Mobile App is a companion to the Inns by the Sea website, built to help travelers easily browse properties, check availability, and book stays directly from their phones. My role encompassed end-to-end product design, from initial discovery to hand-off of polished Figma assets.

Problem Statement
