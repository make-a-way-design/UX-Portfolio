Inns by the Sea — Mobile Booking Experience

A concise, image‑first case study demonstrating a user‑centered redesign of the booking flow — focused on clarity, discoverability of gift certificates, and a smoother path to reservation.

RoleUX Designer — Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Testing
TimelineOct 2024 – Jun 2025
ToolsFigma, Interviews, Usability Tests
GoalReduce booking friction & clarify gift certificate purchase

Problem

Guests struggled to locate gift certificates and encountered ambiguity in the booking flow, creating friction and missed conversions.

Process

  • Research — 5 interviews + usability tests surfaced booking pain points
  • Define — Personas & task flows guided the redesign
  • Design — Wireframes → interactive prototype in Figma
  • Test — Iterated flows to reduce confusion & steps

Solution

  • Gift certificates accessible from primary navigation
  • Simplified booking flow with fewer steps
  • Responsive, mobile‑first UI with clear visual hierarchy

Results

  • Booking task success rate improved in usability tests
  • Significant reduction in confusion about gift certificates
  • Clearer, faster path to reservations on mobile

Reflection

Early, lightweight testing surfaced critical issues quickly. Iteration aligned user needs with business goals and informed a concise, scalable mobile pattern for future features.
