Email Campaign Case Study

Inns by the Sea

A focused series of email marketing campaigns designed to drive weekend bookings and promote key offerings. This case study highlights campaign objectives, creative iterations, and performance.

Channel: Email Only

Strategy & Approach

  • Objective: Increase direct bookings and package awareness through targeted emails.
  • Segmentation: Recent visitors, seasonal travelers, and late summer bookings.
  • Cadence: 1-2 sends per month.
  • Creative System: Consistent templates with modular hero, offer, and CTA blocks.
  • Metrics Tracked: Open Rate, CTR, Conversion Rate, Revenue per Send.
